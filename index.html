What is Magic: The Gathering?

Magic: The Gathering (MTG) is a collectible card game where powerful wizards—called Planeswalkers—battle using decks filled with spells, creatures, and magical artifacts. Each match combines strategy, creativity, and knowledge of thousands of unique cards.

Goal of the Game

Reduce your opponent’s life total from 20 to 0 using creatures, spells, and abilities. Build decks around your favorite playstyles—aggro, control, combo, or midrange.

How to Play

  • Setup: Each player draws 7 cards.
  • Turn phases: Draw → Main → Combat → End.
  • Mana: Tap lands to cast spells.
  • Win: Bring your opponent’s life to zero.

Card Types

Every card has a role:
Lands create mana, Creatures attack and defend, Instants and Sorceries cast spells, Enchantments and Artifacts give ongoing effects, and Planeswalkers represent allies with unique powers.

Why Semantic Search for MTG?

With over 25,000 cards, finding the right one can be daunting. MAGICSEARCH understands your intent — not just keywords. Search “deal 3 damage to any target” and instantly find cards like Lightning Bolt or Play with Fire — even if you don’t remember their names!
