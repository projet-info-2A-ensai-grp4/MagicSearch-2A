    The 5 Colors of Magic

    Magic is built around five colors of mana, each with unique playstyles, strengths, and philosophies.

    W

    White

    Philosophy: Order, protection, justice

    Strengths: Life gain, small creatures, removing threats

    Weaknesses: Card draw, mana ramp

    U

    Blue

    Philosophy: Knowledge, control, perfection

    Strengths: Card draw, countering spells, flying creatures

    Weaknesses: Destroying permanent threats

    B

    Black

    Philosophy: Power, ambition, sacrifice

    Strengths: Creature removal, reanimation, card advantage

    Weaknesses: Enchantment removal, artifacts

    R

    Red

    Philosophy: Freedom, emotion, chaos

    Strengths: Direct damage, fast creatures, artifact destruction

    Weaknesses: Enchantment removal, defense

    G

    Green

    Philosophy: Nature, growth, tradition

    Strengths: Big creatures, mana ramp, enchantment removal

    Weaknesses: Flying creatures, countering spells
